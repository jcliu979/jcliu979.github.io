About

Hi! I'm an 3rd year undergraduate at the University of Texas at Austin, majoring in computer science and mathematics.

Alongside research, I've also joined Freetail Hackers as a member of the tech team, engineering full stack elements regarding the static webpage and the interface used to manage hacker applications.
