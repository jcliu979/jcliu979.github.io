About

Hi! I'm an 3rd year undergraduate at the University of Texas at Austin, majoring in computer science and mathematics.

At UT, I've kept myself busy through research, working at VITA Group under Dr. Atlas Wang and several amazing postdocs and grad students. I'm excited at the chance to learn at my upcoming internships with Amazon and Google!
